Frequently Asked Questions

How can I start learning English on this website?

You can start by exploring our beginner lessons, interactive exercises, and quizzes. We also offer structured courses to guide you step by step.

Is this website free to use?

Yes, free for basic features. Paid plan needed for advanced lessons.

Do I need to create an account?

An account is not required for basic lessons, but yes, you need one for advanced learning.

How can I build my English vocabulary?

Practice regularly by learning new words from lessons, reading, and using interactive exercises. Reviewing and using words in sentences helps retention.

Do you offer certificates for completed courses?

Yes, we provide certificates for completed courses. These certificates can help you showcase your learning progress and achievements, and are especially useful for resumes, job applications, or personal development records.
